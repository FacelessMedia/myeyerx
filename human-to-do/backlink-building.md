## Tier 4: HARO / Connectively (Expert Sourcing)

Journalists need expert quotes. You can get mentions in news articles.

### Setup:
1. Sign up at [https://www.connectively.us](https://www.connectively.us) (formerly HARO)
2. Select categories: **Health/Medical**, **Automotive**, **Business**
3. You'll receive daily emails with journalist queries

### When to Respond:
Look for queries about:
- Window tint / car modifications
- Telehealth / telemedicine
- Eye health / optometry
- Small business / entrepreneurship
- Medical technology
- Driving safety

### Response Template:
