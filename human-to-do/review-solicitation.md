# Review Solicitation Strategy

**Time needed**: 30 min setup, then 5 min per request
**Priority**: HIGH — Google reviews directly impact local rankings and click-through rates
**Goal**: 25+ Google reviews within 60 days

---

## Step 1: Create Your Review Link

1. Go to [Google Business Profile Manager](https://business.google.com/)
2. Click **Home** → **Get more reviews** (or **Ask for reviews**)
3. Copy the short review link — it looks like: `https://g.page/r/YOUR_ID/review`
4. Save this link — you'll use it everywhere

If you can't find it:
1. Search "MyEyeRx" on Google Maps
2. Click your listing
3. Click "Write a review"
4. Copy that URL from your browser

---

## Step 2: Email Template for Past Patients

Send this to your last 50+ patients. Personalize the [NAME] field.

**Subject**: Quick favor, [NAME]? (30 seconds)

```
Hi [NAME],

Thank you for choosing MyEyeRx for your window tint exemption. I hope your 
certificate has been working well for you!

I have a quick favor to ask — would you be willing to leave us a brief Google 
review? It takes about 30 seconds and helps other patients with similar 
conditions find our service.

Just click here: [YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK]

Even a simple "Great service, got my exemption quickly" makes a big difference 
for us.

Thank you so much!

Tory Dewberry
Founder, MyEyeRx
(734) 644-1804
```

## Step 3: Text Message Template (Higher Response Rate)

If you have patients' phone numbers and permission to text:

```
Hi [NAME]! This is Tory from MyEyeRx. Hope your tint exemption 
is working great! Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? 
It really helps: [YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK] Thank you! 🙏
```

---

## Step 4: Timing Strategy

- **Best time to ask**: 3-7 days after they receive their certificate (they're happiest then)
- **Second best**: Send a batch to past patients now
- **Ongoing**: Set up an automated email that goes out 5 days after certificate delivery

### Batch Schedule:
| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Email 15 most recent patients |
| Week 2 | Email next 15 patients |
| Week 3 | Email next 15 patients |
| Week 4 | Text/email any non-responders from week 1 |
| Ongoing | Email every new patient 5 days after delivery |

---

## Step 5: Respond to EVERY Review

This is critical — Google sees businesses that respond to reviews as more active and trustworthy.

### For Positive Reviews (5 stars):
```
Thank you so much, [NAME]! We're glad we could help you get your 
window tint exemption quickly and easily. Drive safely! — Tory, MyEyeRx
```

### For Neutral Reviews (3-4 stars):
```
Thank you for your feedback, [NAME]. We're always looking to improve 
our service. If there's anything we could have done better, please 
don't hesitate to reach out to me directly at Tory@myeyerx.net. 
— Tory, MyEyeRx
```

### For Negative Reviews (1-2 stars):
```
[NAME], I'm sorry to hear about your experience. I'd like to make 
this right. Please contact me directly at Tory@myeyerx.net or 
(734) 644-1804 so I can look into this personally. — Tory Dewberry, 
Founder of MyEyeRx
```

> **Never argue** with a negative review publicly. Always take it offline.

---

## Step 6: Add Review Link to Touchpoints

Put your Google review link in these places:
- [ ] Email signature
- [ ] Post-purchase confirmation email
- [ ] Certificate delivery email
- [ ] Website footer (optional: "Rate us on Google")
- [ ] Business cards / printed materials
- [ ] Social media bios

---

## What NOT to Do

- **Never offer incentives** for reviews (violates Google's TOS, can get your GBP suspended)
- **Never ask for "5-star" reviews** — just ask for "an honest review"
- **Never create fake reviews** — Google detects and penalizes this
- **Never review-gate** (only sending review links to happy customers based on a survey)
