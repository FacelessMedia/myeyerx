## Video 1: Process Explainer (MOST IMPORTANT)

### What to Film:
A 2-3 minute walkthrough of how MyEyeRx works.

### Script Outline:
```
[Intro - 15 sec]
"Hi, I'm Tory Dewberry, founder of MyEyeRx. If you have a medical 
condition that makes you sensitive to light, you may qualify for a 
medical window tint exemption. Let me show you how easy it is."

[The Problem - 20 sec]
"Every state has limits on how dark your window tint can be. But if 
you have conditions like migraines, lupus, photosensitivity, or 
post-surgical eye sensitivity, those limits can make driving 
uncomfortable or even dangerous."

[The Solution - 30 sec]
"MyEyeRx connects you with a licensed optometrist — Dr. Elizabeth 
Borowiec — for an online evaluation. No office visit needed. You'll 
answer some questions about your condition, and if you qualify, 
you'll receive a physician-signed exemption certificate within 
24 to 48 hours."

[How It Works - 45 sec]
"Here's the process:
Step 1: Visit myeyerx.net and select your state.
Step 2: Complete a brief medical questionnaire.
Step 3: A licensed physician reviews your case.
Step 4: If approved, your signed certificate is delivered by email.
That's it. The whole process takes about 10-15 minutes on your end."

[Trust Signals - 20 sec]
"We're HIPAA compliant, our evaluations are done by a licensed 
optometrist, and we serve patients in 42 states. Prices start 
at $225 depending on your state."

[CTA - 15 sec]
"Visit myeyerx.net to check if you qualify. Link's in the description."
```

### Filming Tips:
- Film in a clean, well-lit space (natural light is best)
- Wear professional attire
- Use your phone in landscape mode on a tripod/stand
- Look at the camera lens, not the screen
- Speak clearly and at a natural pace
- Film 2-3 takes and pick the best one

## Video 2-5: Patient Testimonials

### How to Get Testimonial Videos:

**Option A: Ask satisfied patients directly**
When a patient reaches out to thank you, ask:
```
"That's great to hear! Would you be willing to record a short 
30-second video about your experience? It would really help other 
people in your situation find our service. You can just use your 
phone — no need to be fancy!"
```

**Option B: Send a follow-up email to past patients**
```
Subject: Would you share your experience? (30 seconds)

Hi [NAME],

We'd love to hear how your window tint exemption has been working 
for you! Would you be willing to record a quick 30-second video 
testimonial on your phone?

Just answer these 3 questions:
1. What condition do you have and how did it affect your driving?
2. How was your experience with MyEyeRx?
3. Would you recommend MyEyeRx to others?

You can email the video to Tory@myeyerx.net or text it to 
(734) 644-1804.

Thank you!
Tory
```

**Option C: Film them yourself (if local)**
If a patient is in the Detroit/Dearborn area, offer to meet them and film a quick testimonial.

### What Makes a Good Testimonial:
- Patient mentions their **specific condition** (migraines, lupus, etc.)
- Patient mentions the **state** they're in
- Patient mentions how **easy/fast** the process was
- Patient shows or mentions the **certificate** working with police/DMV
- Genuine emotion — don't script it too heavily
