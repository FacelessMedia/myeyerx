# Social Media Content Strategy

**Time needed**: 2-3 hours/week for content creation + posting
**Priority**: MEDIUM — Builds brand awareness, drives referral traffic, signals activity to Google
**Goal**: 3 posts/week across platforms

---

## Platforms & Priority

| Platform | Priority | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| TikTok | HIGH | Short-form video (30-60 sec) | 3x/week |
| Instagram | HIGH | Reels + Stories + Posts | 3x/week |
| YouTube | HIGH | Long-form + Shorts | 1 long/month + 3 Shorts/week |
| Facebook | MEDIUM | Posts + shared articles | 2x/week |

---

## Content Pillars (Rotate These)

### 1. Educational (40% of posts)
- Window tint laws explained
- What VLT percentage means
- How medical exemptions work
- State-specific tint rules
- Qualifying conditions explained

### 2. Patient Stories / Social Proof (25% of posts)
- Testimonials (video or text overlay)
- Before/after scenarios
- "Day in the life" with tint exemption
- Success stories

### 3. Tips & How-To (20% of posts)
- How to apply for an exemption
- What to do if pulled over
- How to keep your exemption current
- Tips for choosing tint darkness

### 4. Brand / Behind the Scenes (15% of posts)
- Meet Tory / Meet Dr. Borowiec
- Why MyEyeRx was founded
- HIPAA compliance and trust
- New state launches

## Tools to Make This Easier

- **Canva**: Create graphics, text overlays, branded templates (free tier works)
- **CapCut**: Edit short-form videos (free, great for TikTok/Reels)
- **Later.com or Buffer**: Schedule posts in advance (free tier: 30 posts/month)
- **Google Trends**: Check what tint-related topics are trending

## Repurpose Content (Work Smarter)

Every blog article on myeyerx.net can become:
1. A TikTok/Reel (summarize in 30-60 sec)
2. An Instagram carousel (5-7 slides of key points)
3. A Facebook post (share with excerpt)
4. A YouTube Short (same as TikTok)
5. A GBP post (summary + link)

That's 5 pieces of content from 1 article.
