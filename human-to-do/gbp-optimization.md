# Google Business Profile Optimization — Step-by-Step

**Time needed**: ~2-3 hours total
**Priority**: HIGH — GBP directly affects local search rankings and trust signals

---

## Step 1: Fix Business Name

1. Go to [Google Business Profile Manager](https://business.google.com/)
2. Click **Edit profile** → **Business information** → **About**
3. Change **Business name** from "My Eye Rx" to **MyEyeRx** (no space)
4. Save

> **Why**: Brand name consistency across website, GBP, social media, and citations is a ranking factor. Google cross-references your name everywhere.

---

## Step 2: Fix Phone Number (CRITICAL)

1. Go to **Edit profile** → **Contact**
2. Change phone from **(734) 338-8453** to **(734) 644-1804**
3. Save

> **Why**: NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency is one of the top 5 local SEO ranking factors. Your website says (734) 644-1804 but GBP says (734) 338-8453. This mismatch hurts rankings everywhere.

## Step 8: Add Opening Date

1. Go to **Edit profile** → **Business information** → **About** → **Opening date**
2. Enter the date MyEyeRx launched (the year is most important)
3. Save

> Businesses with longer histories rank higher. Don't leave this blank.
